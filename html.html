Festival Orientation

How Cannes Works & Access

Cannes spans multiple sections centred on the Palais. The Official Selection hosts gala red-carpet premieres in the Grand Théâtre Lumière, while sidebars like Un Certain Regard and Directors’ Fortnight showcase emerging voices.

Festival Window 12 Days • Mid May
Primary Venues Palais & Croisette
Access Routes Accreditation • Hosted

Accreditation vs. Hosted Hospitality

  • Accreditation is required for professional areas (industry, press, Marché du Film). Applications open in winter.
  • There is no general public box office for gala premieres.
  • Hosted hospitality via Above + Beyond is the practical route for red-carpet evenings and high-demand events without industry credentials.
Not sure which path fits? We advise on accreditation routes and secure hosted alternatives when needed.
